About Me

Hi, I’m Amelie, a multidisciplinary designer who loves mixing creativity with strategy to help brands connect with people in meaningful, genuine ways.

I studied digital arts and have a passion for blending tech and design to create visuals that feel fresh, fun, and thoughtful. From brand identities and websites to content creation and interactive projects, I’m all about clean, intentional design that tells a story and sparks connection.

When I’m not designing, you’ll probably find me painting at my easel or capturing moments with my camera.
