About Me

Hi, I am Amelie, a multidisciplinary designer. My approach combines strategic thinking with creative execution to help brands connect with their audiences in authentic ways.

I'm a digital arts graduate with a passion for blending creativity and technology to craft engaging visual experiences. My work spans across brand identity, web design, editorial layouts, and interactive media, with a focus on clean aesthetics and purposeful design. I'm driven by curiosity, storytelling, and the desire to create work that connects and resonates.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring photography, hiking in nature, or experimenting new painting techniques.
