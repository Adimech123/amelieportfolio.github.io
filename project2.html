Brand Identity Design

All social media content and poster designs

Client
Student Organisation
Duration
2 Years
Role
Head PRO

Project Overview

I was responsible for planning, creating, and delegating content for our social media platforms.

My role involved ensuring our content adhered to a consistent retro style while remaining interesting and informative. I maintained a regular posting schedule to keep students engaged with our events, which was crucial given the constant competition with other organisations. I collaborated closely with my team to create content that best represented our brand and values.

This work was extremely rewarding, as it gave me the opportunity to experiment with design and deepen my understanding of posting and creating online content.

Design Process

Skills & Tools

This work encouraged exploration and creativity within a structured framework, which was a new concept to me.

The role involved transforming creative poster designs into content that was both informative and easy to read. Posts also needed to be adaptable across multiple platforms. Planning and creating Reels opened a new area of interest and helped me develop valuable new skills.
